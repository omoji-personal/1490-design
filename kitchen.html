The 9-element kitchen signature

  • Light oak Shaker base cabinets (KraftMaid Vantage tier — premium semi-custom)
  • Marble counters: Statuario waterfall (not subway tile, not standard quartz)
  • One warm-wood gesture: the oak island is THE wood gesture; floor stays porcelain, not oak
  • Lacquered brass hardware mixed with matte black (5:1 brass:black ratio)
  • Brass pendant lighting — Rejuvenation Pinnock Cone Aged Brass × 3 (canon-compliant successor to discontinued Schoolhouse Hyatt; owner reselect pending — see K-PENDANTS)
  • Backsplash slab Carrara (range wall) — bookmatched-look continuity to counter
  • Cle Sea Salt zellige (counter-to-upper elsewhere) — texture without color
  • One real plant at floor height — kentia palm or bird of paradise (cat-safe)
  • Restrained ceramic groupings on open shelf above sink — 3 pieces max
Kitchen kill list:
  • NO subway tile backsplash
  • NO white shaker with chrome hardware (modern farmhouse adjacent)
  • NO open shelving carrying matched-set dinnerware
  • NO industrial pendants (no Edison cage, no cone metal-shade)
  • NO painted accent island (the wood IS the accent)
  • NO butcher block counter (no, even on island)
  • NO walnut cabinetry (oak is the wood; walnut is accent only and not in kitchen)
