OWIU Studio

LA Japandi precision. Palmero, Ryokan, Echo Park, Duane, Brentwood, Wellington Office are canon. Biscuit Loft + Glass Ridge are NOT.

The LA Japandi voice. OWIU Studio brings the precision and warmth-from-wood that anchors California Modern Japandi. Only WARM projects.

Warm projects (canon)

  • Palmero House (Atwater Village) — kitchen anchor
  • Ryokan House — bath + entry anchor
  • Echo Park Hill House — LR + dining
  • Duane House — full-house warm Japandi
  • Brentwood Residence — kitchen + LR boundary of "warm enough"
  • Wellington Office — office built-in inspiration

Rejected projects (do NOT use)

OWIU projects that are NOT canon (too cold/austere/loft-industrial):
  • Biscuit Loft — too pale, lacks coziness, reads industrial
  • Glass Ridge — too minimal-to-the-point-of-clinical
The boundary is "warm enough." If a project reads austere, it's not us.
