## Sourcing notes

- studiolifestyle.net redirects to an unrelated Italian branding agency; the real US firm lives at studio-lifestyle.com (Shopify-hosted)
- Studio LIFE/STYLE has two distinct Brentwood projects on their site (`/brentwood` and `/brentwood-2`); only `brentwood-2` matches the brief
- Allprace site (Squarespace) serves images up to 2500w via `?format=2500w` query — all 4 allprace_*.jpg files are pulled at that resolution (~500-700 KB each)
- Studio LIFE/STYLE Brentwood-2 images are only published at ~1024-1500 wide; pulled originals as-is (~150-345 KB each). Source masters not exposed.
- No scraping blocks encountered; standard browser User-Agent was sufficient for both domains
