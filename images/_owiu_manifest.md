## Aesthetic anchors (consistent across all 12)
Light oak wide-plank floors. Plaster / cream / soft-white walls. Warm wood accents.
Marble counters where kitchen/bath. Lacquered brass hardware + brass lighting.
Cream linen anchor furniture. Real plants. Restrained color accent per room. Hand-thrown ceramics.

## Notes / caveats

- Room labels (kitchen/living/dining/bath/bedroom/entry) are inferred from OWIU's typical project image-sequence pattern; OWIU does not caption images on their site. Quick eyeball pass by the user is advised before publishing to verify each label.
- "Ryokan House" in the brief refers to OWIU's Mount Washington Ray Kappe mid-century home (the Ryokan-aesthetic project per Interior Design magazine). That project does NOT appear on the current owiu-design.com /work/residential index — it may live under arts-and-culture or have been retired. Echo Park Hill House (2024) is the closest active Ryokan-aesthetic project and is heavily represented here (slots 04-06).
- Wellington Office stands in for Brentwood / additional living context (slot 12) since the Brentwood gallery only had 9 interior images and we needed a 5-project spread.
- All images served from `cms.owiu-design.com` (WordPress). The OWIU site itself is Next.js at `www.owiu-design.com`. The brief's URL `owiustudio.com` does not resolve (NXDOMAIN) — correct domain is `owiu-design.com`. Instagram handle per public press is `@owiu_design`.
