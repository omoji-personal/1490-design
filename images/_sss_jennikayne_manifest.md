## Sourcing Notes

- jennikayne.com (Shopify) returns a soft-404 to non-browser User-Agents on `/blogs/ripandtan/*` URLs (redirects to `/em-cgi/btag/...`) — body images on the official article pages were not surfaced by curl. Remodelista's editorial feature carries the same Tessa Neustadt photo set at 1466w high-res and was used as the canonical source.
- Architectural Digest URLs for both designers 404 against curl with browser UA; AD imagery was not sourced this pass.
- All 10 images served HTTP 200 with browser UA + referer; no placeholders required.

## Aesthetic Match Confirmation

All 10 images embody: light oak floors, plaster/cream walls, warm wood, marble counters (Carrara/Calacatta), lacquered brass, cream linen sofas, real plants, restrained color accents, hand-thrown ceramics. No dark walnut, saturated walls, modern farmhouse, or cold/austere imagery in the set.
