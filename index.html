The 9-Element Composite Signature

  1. Light oak wide-plank floor
  2. Plaster, cream, or soft-white walls — never saturated
  3. One warm-wood gesture per surface (oak island OR built-in OR coffee table — not all)
  4. Marble counters + backsplash — bookmatched or slab; not subway
  5. Brass hardware + at least one brass light fixture per public room
  6. One cream linen upholstered anchor per LR (never patterned, never tufted)
  7. One real plant tree at floor height per public room (cat-safe substitutions)
  8. One "real color" accent per room — sage, mustard, rust, or olive-green
  9. Hand-thrown ceramics in restrained groupings (3-5 per shelf)
