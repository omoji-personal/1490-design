Boucle

Crypton
Stain/spill-resistant performance fabric — wipe-clean, pet-safe, washable. The practical choice for high-use pieces.
Thermostatic
Shower valve that holds your set temperature regardless of pressure fluctuations (so no scalding when someone flushes). More expensive than pressure-balance but more comfortable.
GREENGUARD Gold
Low-VOC emissions certification for indoor air quality — required for nursery furniture per our spec.
CFM
Cubic Feet per Minute — airflow measure for range hoods. Higher = more powerful extraction.
